Research

My research is focused on reducing bias in healthcare ML models. We are specifially bias models for OUD or opioid use disorder.

These biases can affect marginalized groups by concluding that Black patients are healthier than equally sick White patients, falsly predicting heart disease probabilities, assigning black women a higher chance of having postpartum depression, and more. We plan on creating a custom model to reduce bias.

We are looking at work like FairEHR-CLP, which uses generative models to do contrastive learning on EHR data.
